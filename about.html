About
Bike world offers a wide variety of options for our members, because bike world was a biking club before it became a biking gym. For our members that enjoy riding outdoors we have two options: spin classes on our rooftop terrace or our weekly trips to various biking trails around the state. Our members have the option to bring their own bike or use one of ours for every adventure. If by chance the outdoors is not your speed don't worry we have plenty of temperature controled rooms where you can either partake in a spin class or just enjoy your own ride. To see some of previous adventures check out our Gallery.
